# False-Alarm Scenarios (Class: `false_alarm`)

Three scenarios with high sensor activation but NO fire hazard.

## C1: Cooking Fumes (`false_alarm_cooking`)

**Setup:** frying or high-heat cooking for 5 minutes

**Expected:** smoke 250–500 ppm, flame 300–600 ADC (gas) or 50–150 (electric), temp +1–2°C, CO <10 ppm, VOC 150–350 ppm

**Collection:** 30 × 10 s samples

---

## C2: Steam (`false_alarm_steam`)

**Setup:** kettle or hot shower near sensors

**Expected:** smoke 80–150 ppm (moisture particles), flame 50–150 ADC, temp +0.5–1.5°C, humidity 45%→70–85%, CO <3 ppm, VOC <40 ppm

**Collection:** 30 × 10 s samples

---

## C3: Aerosol Sprays / Cleaning Products / Alcohol Vapors (`false_alarm_voc_spray`)

**Setup:** spray air freshener or disinfectant near sensors

**Expected:** smoke 150–300 ppm (spray particulates), flame 50–150 ADC, temp stable, CO <2 ppm, VOC 300–500 ppm

**Collection:** 30 × 10 s samples (spray + decay)
